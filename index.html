Croque madam by Alex

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Milk 100ml
  • Bread 2 slices
  • Cheese 2 slices
  • Ham 2 slices
  • Butter
  • Mustard
  • Spices
  • Oil

Method

  1. Cut two slices of bread 1 cm
  2. Grease each slice with butter, and then with mustard
  3. Put 2 layers of ham and 2 layers of cheese on the first piece of bread, and put on the second piece of bread
  4. Mix one egg with milk, season with spices and dip the sandwich on both sides
  5. Heat the oil in a pan and fry until golden brown on top and bottom
  6. For the top, prepare the sunny-side up from one egg
